What is CritiqueKit?

CritiqueKit is a system and tool that helps students give better feedback to peers. Feedback is a critical part of learning, especially in creative domains. However, good feedback is rare because it is time-consuming to give and people are not consistently skilled at providing it. This project uses technological interventions such as task structuring and just-in-time guidance to help people give better feedback. We are actively developing an online peer feedback system for use in both online and in-person classrooms that embodies these techniques.


How does CritiqueKit work?
